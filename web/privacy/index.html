This Privacy Policy explains how Chengdu Yuliu Technology Co., Ltd. (“Yuliu Tech”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the YOLO HAPPY iOS application (the “App”).

1. Data we process

  • Account data (email, display name, nationality preferences) when you sign in
  • Trip preferences and itineraries you create in the App
  • Purchase and subscription status processed via Apple App Store and RevenueCat (we do not receive your full payment card details)

  • Support messages if you contact Genius Bar / support

2. How we use data

  • Provide guides, itineraries, membership entitlements, and account features
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Respond to support requests and improve product quality
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing

We use service providers such as Supabase (backend), Apple (App Store / Sign in with Apple), and RevenueCat (subscription validation). They process data only as needed to provide their services.

4. Retention & your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by emailing support@yolohappy.app. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings.
